Baking a Cake

Step by Step Instructions

Read Left to RIght

Gather Ingredients

First Step to baking a cake is going to be gathering all the necessary ingredients which include:
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 of unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 and 3/4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Starting the Process

  • First thing is to preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Next, grab a 9 or 10 inch square cake pan.
  • Proceed to grease it down with either some oil or some extra butter and some flour to the bottom of the pan.
  • Now it's time to begin combining ingredients.

Combining Wet Ingredients

Lets begin combining the wet ingredients
  • Grab a mixing bowl.
  • Gather sugar and butter then proceed to put them into the bowl.
  • Begin mixing the two ingredients inside of the bowl.
  • Now, grab 1 egg, crack it open, and put the yolk into the bowl.
  • Begin mixing the ingredients some more.
  • Add the last egg into the bowl using the process from before.
  • Add the vanilla extract into the bowl.
  • Mix all of the ingredients together.

Combining Dry Ingredients

Combining dry ingredients with the wet ones
  • Grab flour and pour into the bowl.
  • Grab the baking powder and add into the bowl.
  • Begin mixing all ingredients together.
  • Finally add milk.
  • Keep mixing all the ingredients until smooth.

The Baking Process

  • After all ingredients are smoothed out, pour into the already greased up pan.
  • Spread ingredients evenly throughout pan until it is filled.
  • Now open up the preheated oven and proceed to put the cake pan into it.
  • Close the oven door and let the cake sit for 30-40 minutes.
  • After the 30-40 minutes, take the cake out of the oven.
  • Now it's time to add frosting and flavors.

Adding frosting and flavors

  • Now that the cake is fully cooked, wait for it to cool down.
  • Gather any flavor frosting that you desire.
  • Pour the frosting onto the cake and spread evenly on top.
  • If you have any sprinkles or additional ingredients you would like to add to the top, now is the time to add them.
  • Enjoy!
